# Lab 04 - MVP Feature List

## MVP Decision Rule
The MVP should include the smallest set of features that can demonstrate the core problem-solution fit by the end of the semester.

## MoSCoW Prioritization

| Feature ID | Feature Name | Problem Solved | Related User Story | Priority (Must/Should/Could/Won't) | Technical Complexity (1-5) | User Value (1-5) | Evidence Strength (1-5) | Include in Final Prototype? |
|---|---|---|---|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| F01 | User Login | Users need a secure way to access the system and manage their bookings. | US-01 | Must | 2 | 5 | 5 | Yes | 
| F02 | View Available Rooms | Students cannot easily determine room availability. | US-02 | Must | 3 | 5 | 5 | Yes | 
| F03 | Search Rooms by Date and Time | Students spend time asking staff for available rooms. | US-03 | Must | 3 | 5 | 5 | Yes | 
| F04 | Book a Room | Current booking process requires paper forms and office visits. | US-04 | Must | 4 | 5 | 5 | Yes | 
| F05 | View My Bookings | Users need to track their reservations. | US-05 | Must | 2 | 4 | 4 | Yes | 
| F06 | Cancel Booking | Users need flexibility if plans change. | US-06 | Should | 2 | 4 | 4 | Yes | 
| F07 | Manage Rooms | Staff need to add, edit, or remove rooms from the system. | US-07 | Should | 3 | 4 | 4 | Yes | 
| F08 | Multilingual Support (Thai/English) | International students face language barriers. | US-08 | Could | 2 | 4 | 5 | If time permits | 
| F09 | Booking Approval Workflow | Staff may want to approve room requests manually. | US-09 | Won't | 4 | 3 | 2 | No | 
| F10 | Email Notifications | Users receive booking reminders and confirmations. | US-10 | Won't | 4 | 3 | 2 | No | 
| F11 | Calendar View | Users can visualize room schedules. | US-11 | Could | 3 | 3 | 3 | If time permits | 
| F12 | QR Code Check-in | Verify room usage after booking. | US-12 | Won't | 5 | 2 |

## Must-Have Features 
- User Login
- View Available Rooms
- Search Rooms by Date and Time
- Book a Room
- View My Bookings

## Should-Have Features
- Cancel Booking
- Manage Rooms (Admin/Staff)

## Could-Have / Future Features
- Multilingual Support (Thai/English)
- Calendar View for room schedules

## Not in MVP
- The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP because they introduce additional complexity, require more development time, or are not essential for validating the core problem-solution fit:
- Booking approval workflow
- Email notifications
- QR code check-in
- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Mobile application support
