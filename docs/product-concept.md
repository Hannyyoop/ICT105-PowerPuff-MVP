# Product Concept

## 1. Project Title
Smart Study Room Booking System

## 2. Target User
Primary user: Students who need to reserve study rooms for individual study or group discussions.

## 3. Problem Statement
Students need an easier way to check study room availability and make reservations because the current process is manual, time-consuming, and can lead to booking conflicts.ages, and manual office visits.

## 4. Proposed IT Product
A web-based study room booking system that allows students to view available study rooms, search by date and time, make reservations, view their booking status, and allows library staff to manage room bookings and availability.

## 5. Core Value Proposition
The system makes study room reservations faster, prevents double bookings, provides real-time room availability, and reduces the workload for library staff.

## 6. MVP Scope
The MVP will focus on viewing available study rooms, searching rooms by date, time, and capacity , booking a study room , viewing booking status , preventing double bookings and library staff booking management dashboard.

## 7. Out of Scope for Current Semester
- QR code check-in and check-out
- Email or SMS notifications
- Integration with the university timetable
- Mobile application development
- AI-based room recommendations
