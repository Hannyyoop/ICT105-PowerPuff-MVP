## Reflection
### Which feature creates the strongest user value?
F-02 (Available Rooms) combined with F-04 (Book a Room) creates the highest user value. It directly solves the students' primary frustration of having to physically walk to offices, fill out paper forms in Thai, or guess which rooms are currently empty by offering immediate visibility and instant booking.

### Which feature creates the strongest business or operational value?
F-05 (My Bookings & Cancellation) provides the best operational value. By giving students the power to cancel their own reservations effortlessly, the system automatically recycles freed-up slots back into the availability pool. This minimizes ghost bookings and maximizes campus facility utilization without staff intervention.

### Which required feature is still weak or unclear?
F-06 (Manage Rooms / Admin Control) remains the least detailed. While the system needs a way for staff to add or close rooms, the exact workflow of how campus administrators verify student identities, handle booking conflicts, or lock rooms for special events has not been fully mapped out in the current student-focused MVP.
