# Experiment Script

## 1. Opening Script
Thank you for helping us test our prototype. This is not a test of you; there are no right or wrong answers. We are testing whether our product idea and user interface are clear and easy to navigate. Please think aloud and share your honest thoughts while using the prototype.

## 2. Test Scenario
You are a university student who needs to find and reserve a quiet study space/meeting room for a group project this afternoon. You want to see what is open, book a slot, and make sure your request goes through.

## 3. Tasks for Tester
| Task No. | Task Instruction | Related Requirement | Expected Result |
|---|---|---|---|

| **T1** | Open the homepage and explain what this system is for and what you can do here. | FR-01 (Discovery Dashboard) | User immediately understands it is a campus room booking app. |

| **T2** | Find an available study room and select a time slot for this afternoon. | FR-02 (Calendar Scheduling View) | User filters/browses rooms and selects a valid time slot. |

| **T3** | Fill out and submit the booking application form. | FR-03 (Booking Form Submission) | User completes the required details and sees a success confirmation screen. |

| **T4** | Access your booking history to find the room details you just reserved. | FR-04 (Reservation Detail View) | User opens the specific booking record and reviews the room details. |

| **T5** | Check the approval or verification status of your room reservation. | FR-05 (Reservation Status Tracking) | User correctly identifies whether the booking is pending, approved, or rejected. |

## 4. Observation Notes
Record specific behavioral indicators during the test session:
- **Hesitations:** Where does the tester pause or look confused (e.g., during time-slot selection or language switching)?
- **Misclicks:** Which non-clickable elements do they try to press?
- **Questions Asked:** What clarity or functional details do they actively ask the moderator for?
- **Verbal Feedback:** Any direct positive or negative comments expressed while thinking aloud.

## 5. Closing Questions
- What parts of the booking process were the easiest to complete?
- Was any terminology or layout step confusing (especially around time selection or status tracking)?
- Which feature did you find most useful for solving your study space issues?
- What is one critical thing we should improve or fix before building the final web application?
