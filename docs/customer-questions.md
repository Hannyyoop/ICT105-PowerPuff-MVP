# Customer Discovery Questions

## Project Title

Room Booking System

## Target Respondent

* University students (local and international)
* Administrative staff

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT105 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will not collect unnecessary personal data. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Tell us about the last time you needed to reserve a room or study space.
4. What were you trying to do at that time?
5. What happened from start to finish?

## Current Workaround

6. How do you usually book a room or study space?
7. Do you have to visit the office or contact staff to make a reservation?
8. What difficulties, delays, or frustrations have you experienced with the current booking process?
9. How easy is it for you to obtain information about room availability?
10. Is the current booking form easy for you to understand?
11. Does the use of Thai language only on the booking form create difficulties for you?
12. How clear and effective is communication with staff during the booking process?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

13. How often do you need to reserve a room or study space?
14. On a scale of 1–5, how satisfied are you with the current room booking process? Why?
15. How serious is the problem of having to visit the office or rely on staff to make reservations?
16. What happens if you are unable to reserve a room when you need one?
17. Have you ever experienced delays or inconvenience when booking a room?

## Future Improvements

18. Would being able to check room availability online save you time?
19. Do you think an online room booking system would make the reservation process more efficient?
20. Would multilingual support (e.g., English) improve accessibility for international students?
21. Would you be willing to use a web-based room booking system if it were available?

## Closing

22. Is there anything else we should understand about the current room booking process?
23. May we use your anonymized response as part of our course project evidence?
